About

  • Courses are made up of modules, and modules are made up of sections.
  • Each course is approximately 4 hours long.
  • Each course contains 4 modules, where each module is approximately 1 hour long.
  • Each section is 3 minutes in length.
  • A section consists of a 2 minute video presentation followed by 3 multiple choice questions.
  • There are 20 sections per module.
  • Students must score 100% in order to pass. If they fail, they will need to sit the course again.
  • It is not possible to skip the video presentations.
  • Students get paid for sitting courses. The amount they get paid depends on the level of course and the rating.
  • The level of the course depends on how valuable it is to society.
  • Courses are built using the built-in course builder.
  • After completing a course you can donate Social Credits to the course builder.
  • You can review the total number of Social Credits the course has received.
  • You can only donate Social Credits to courses that have a score of X stars or above and more than 20 reviews.
